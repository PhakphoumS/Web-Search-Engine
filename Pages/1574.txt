<h2 id="Registration">Registration &amp; Fee Information</h2>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(127, 127, 127);">Registration is required for all
mandatory and optional International Student Orientation events. Please register using the <a href="https://forms.uofi.uic.edu/sec/3425036" target="_blank">online registration form</a>.<br>
</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(127, 127, 127);">All incoming international
students are charged a non-refundable Orientation/Arrival Fee of $149 to their university bill to cover the costs of initial arrival services and programs. However, there is no additional charge for new UIC international students to participate in selected ISO events.</span></p>

<h2 id="GuestInformation">Guest Information</h2>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(127, 127, 127);">Family and guests are welcome at all International Student Orientation events. You may register your guests when completing the online registration form linked above.<br>
