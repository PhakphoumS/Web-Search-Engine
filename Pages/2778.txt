		<h3>Networking</h3><br>
		EVL&rsquo;s research in <a href="/category.php?id=23&related=3">Tele-Immersion</a> and networking has led to the development of a computational and information infrastructure integrating high-performance computers, visualization environments, remote instruments and massive databases via high-speed networks to support advanced applications. Tele-immersion has been an &ldquo;application driver&rdquo; to build, exercise, evaluate and fine-tune advanced, high-performance, broadband networks.<br><br> EVL has conducted <a href="/category.php?id=19&related=5">networking research</a> by participating in the development of national and international testbeds, testing and deploying networking services to research and educational communities, and establishing and managing networking infrastructure.
