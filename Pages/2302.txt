                    <h2 class="node__title node-title"><a href="https://www.ihrp.uic.edu/content/project-websites">Project Websites</a> <span class="print-footnote">[1]</span></h2>
            <span property="dc:title" content="Project Websites" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span>
      
          </header>
  
	
  <p>The IHRP website lists all grants currently active at the Institute and those that have completed their funding cycle.</p>
<p>The nature of some research projects &mdash; that engage communities, recruit participants, and disseminate research materials &mdash; require separate, public-oriented websites. Such projects with current funding are listed below with links to their websites.</p>
<p><a href="https://asthmaaction.ihrp.uic.edu/"><strong>Asthma Action</strong></a> <span class="print-footnote">[2]</span></p>
<p>Asthma Action at Erie is a research project to improve asthma management among low-income children in Chicago.</p>
<p><a href="https://cecdp-ihrp.uic.edu/"><strong>Cancer Education and Career Development Program</strong></a> <span class="print-footnote">[3]</span></p>
<p>This fellowship program prepares promising new scholars to conduct research in the critical field of cancer disparities.</p>
<p><a href="https://co-opchicago.ihrp.uic.edu/"><strong>CO-OP Chicago</strong></a> <span class="print-footnote">[4]</span></p>
<p>This study examines different approaches to improving the oral health of Chicago children.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.fitandstrong.org/"><b>Fit &amp; Strong!</b></a> <span class="print-footnote">[5]</span></p>
<p>Fit &amp; Strong is an intervention proven to increase physical activity and improve mobility among older adults with osteoarthritis.</p>
<p><a href="https://tobacconomics.org/"><strong>Tobacconomics&nbsp;</strong></a> <span class="print-footnote">[6]</span></p>
<p>This program is devoted to improving access to economic research and information that can impact and inform tobacco policy.</p>
