  <p>To flourish in their work, researchers must be able to rely on the experience, skills and knowledge of professionals attuned to their administrative needs and those of their sponsors.&nbsp;</p>
<h2>
	At IHRP, researchers find an experienced team of administration professionals who understand the research enterprise and excel in their roles to sustain it.&nbsp;</h2>
<p>Scientists at IHRP can focus on the substance of their research because they can rely on our Admin Team to ensure that budgets and paperwork are in place with i&rsquo;s dotted and t&rsquo;s crossed. Our dedicated, full-time professionals work together to meet the researcher&rsquo;s non-scientific needs, skillfully and seamlessly, while adhering to university and state policies and procedures.</p>
<h2>
	One Team, One Hub of Services</h2>
<p>Our admin team members quickly learn and adapt to new requirements, skillfully manage competing priorities, and forecast and mitigate potential administrative challenges. The offices of our HR, grants, and communications professionals are within a few strides of each other. This proximity allows easy consultation within the team and coordination for the researchers&rsquo; needs.&nbsp;</p>
<p>In addition to support by <a href="https://www.ihrp.uic.edu/content/scientific-resources-intro">our Scientific Cores</a> <span class="print-footnote">[2]</span>, researchers at IHRP benefit from easy access to our specialists in supporting research:&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Grants and Finance</strong> &mdash; Proposals and grants administration from pre-award to closeout, financial oversight of grant funds and expenditures, compliance monitoring.</p>
<p><strong>Human Resources</strong> &mdash; Guidance and legwork in managing personnel requirements and appointments.</p>
<p><strong>Informational Technology</strong> &mdash; Technical support for software and hardware, data storage, server administration and data security.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Communications</strong> &mdash; Promotion of researchers and their work, and consultation to researchers on projects across the communications spectrum.</p>
<p>In all these areas, our Admin Team ensures that researchers&rsquo; work adhere to best practices, University procedures, and the policies of the University, the State and the Sponsor.&nbsp;</p>
