<h3>Building Music</h3><br>
<div class='entry_content'>
	<strong>Researchers:</strong> <a href='/entry.php?id=244'>Julio Obelleiro</a>, Alberto Garc&iacute;a, Jorge Cano, Pedro Enriquez<br><strong>thanks to:</strong> Carlos Cano, Eurico Moita, Sebastian Alvarez<br><br>
	<strong>URL:</strong> <a href='http://www.playthemagic.com/buildingmusic.html' target='_blank'>http://www.playthemagic.com/buildingmusic.html</a><br><br>
<!-- Begin Entry Description -->
<em>Building Music</em> is a public large scale interactive installation that explores the musicality of different architectural features. By moving the light of a torch (flashlight) along the facade of the building the viewers create musical compositions while lighting the windows. Every window hides a sound and a color and the spectators can play with them from a distance.<br><br>

<em>Building Music</em> has been exhibited at the Urban Art Festival (White Night, Bucharest, 2008) and The Spark Festival of Electronic Music and Arts (Minneapolis, 2009).<br><br>
<!-- End Entry Description -->
