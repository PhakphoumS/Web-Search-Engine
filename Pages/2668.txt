		<h3>Web Art</h3><br>
		In the early &rsquo;90s, EVL collaborated with the National Center for Supercomputing Applications (NCSA), University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign on the interface design and functionality of Mosaic, the earliest web-browser and predecessor to Netscape.<br><br> Since then, projects like <a href="/entry.php?id=1691">The File Room</a>, an illustrated archive of cultural censorship cases, have been created, exhibited and widely distributed via the Internet. Other projects, such as <a href="/entry.php?id=1904">Cryptoclub</a> and <a href="/entry.php?id=1887">Synthecology</a>, focus on the web as a community for artistic collaboration or to facilitate early childhood education in the sciences.
