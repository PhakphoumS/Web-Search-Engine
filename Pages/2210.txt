            <span property="dc:title" content="Physical Environment for Social Sciences Research" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span>
      
          </header>
  
	
  <p>The Institute for Health Research and Policy occupies over 32,000 square feet of the Westside Research Office Building.</p>
<p><img alt="I H R P at U I C, 1747 W. Roosevelt Road, Chicago, IL 60608" src="/files/researcher_image/WROB-2017apr17-416x300.jpg" style="width: 312px; height: 225px; float: right; margin: 6px 14px;" /></p>
<h2>
	Our building&#39;s space was designed entirely with collaborative social sciences research in mind.</h2>
<p>Over two floors, the Institute&rsquo;s physical features include:</p>
<ul>
	<li>
		Two sets of focus group rooms with adjoining observation rooms.</li>
	<li>
		Space for survey research.</li>
	<li>
		Securable space for data collection servers and documents with study participant info.</li>
	<li>
		Plenty of meeting space, including two large conference rooms &mdash; one formal, the other appropriate for workshops.</li>
	<li>
		Many smaller meeting rooms for team meetings and participant conversations.</li>
	<li>
		Offices for UIC researchers with IHRP-administered grants.</li>
	<li>
		Offices and cubicles for their research teams.</li>
</ul>
<p>Located in the Illinois Medical District, on the southeast corner of Wood Street and Roosevelt Road, the Institute is a five- to ten-minute walk from the <a href="https://transportation.uic.edu/buses-and-shuttles/">intracampus bus</a> <span class="print-footnote">[2]</span>, the Wood Street parking lot, the CTA Roosevelt bus (#12) and the CTA Pink Line (Polk Street stop).</p>
<p>Learn about other <a href="https://www.ihrp.uic.edu/content/about-advantages-UIC-researchers">research advantages at IHRP</a> <span class="print-footnote">[3]</span>.</p>
