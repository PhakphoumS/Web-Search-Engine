                    <h2 class="node__title node-title"><a href="https://www.ihrp.uic.edu/content/about-our-fellows-affiliates">Recognizing Excellence Among Our Colleagues</a> <span class="print-footnote">[1]</span></h2>

  <p>Each year the Institute for Health Research and Policy recognizes faculty-scientists&nbsp;and PhD-level researchers&nbsp;who conduct their studies with the support of IHRP.</p>
<h2>
	We honor these UIC researchers for their continued pursuit of research excellence in examining and reducing social&nbsp;determinants of health and improving well-being from a broad, multidisciplinary perspective.</h2>
<p>We also are grateful for their commitment to fostering IHRP&#39;s intellectual community.</p>
<h3>
