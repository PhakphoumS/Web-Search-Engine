          							<h1 class="title">Richard T. Campbell, PhD</h1>
			          
	        
          



<div style="position: relative; width: 63%; float: left;">

  
  <h2>
      Professor Emeritus of Biostatistics and Sociology  </h2>

  <p>
    <p>Dr. Richard Campbell is professor emeritus of biostatistics and sociology. He is a member of IHRP&#39;s Methodology Research Core and an <a href="https://ihrp-webdev.ihrp.uic.edu/content/about-our-fellows-affiliates">IHRP Fellow</a>.</p>
  </p>

  <p>
    <h2>Research Interests</h2>
  </p>
  <p>
    <p>Aging and health, racial, ethnic and socio-economic disparities in health, methodology of longitudinal research, statistical analysis of developmental data</p>
  </p>
